About Sidejobster

SIDEJOBSTER is the premier marketplace and advisory resource for Consumers and Service Providers, designed to support quality home repair work, and fair pricing and terms.

 

 

Perfect Your Paradise
Build Your Business

What SIDEJOBSTER Does

Eliminates Frustration

Without the hassle of researching and calling numerous contractors, outline your project needs in one place and receive immediate responses from available qualified providers in your area. The SIDEJOBSTER platform is easy to use and allows for continuous communications with your service provider through project completion and payment.

Ensures More Successful Projects

By improving efficiencies in project scoping, communications and payment processing, SIDEJOBSTER allows you to focus on what you do best, whether you are a busy property- / home-owner with several other competing priorities in your day or a talented contractor who takes pride in your technical skills and guaranteeing quality work.

Levels the Playing Field

In order to provide their services in the SIDEJOBSTER marketplace, service providers must agree to SIDEJOBSTER's pricing, terms, and payment policies. In return service providers can accept projects as they are posted or bid on projects as timing and schedules permit. Consistency, fairness and flexibility = peace of mind for everyone.
